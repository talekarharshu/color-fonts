A sports car is a small, lightweight, and high-performance vehicle designed for speed, agility, and handling. These cars typically have powerful engines, aerodynamic designs, and low ground clearance, making them ideal for fast acceleration and tight cornering.

A car (automobile) is a motor vehicle designed for transporting passengers. It typically has four wheels, an internal combustion engine or electric motor, and is used for personal, commercial, and public transportation. Cars are available in a variety of shapes, sizes, and styles to suit different needs and preferences.
